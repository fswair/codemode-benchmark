--- Snippet 1 [normal]: Typical happy-path example (3 edits) ---
Code: levenshtein("kitten", "sitting")
Output: 3

--- Snippet 2 [normal]: Distance between two empty strings (boundary case) ---
Code: levenshtein("", "")
Output: 0

--- Snippet 3 [normal]: Distance between a string and an empty string (all deletions) ---
Code: levenshtein("abc", "")
Output: 3

--- Snippet 4 [normal]: Distance between empty string and a string (all insertions) ---
Code: levenshtein("", "abc")
Output: 3

--- Snippet 5 [normal]: Distance between identical strings (0 edits) ---
Code: levenshtein("abc", "abc")
Output: 0

--- Snippet 6 [normal]: One string is a prefix of the other (2 insertions) ---
Code: levenshtein("abc", "abcde")
Output: 2

--- Snippet 7 [normal]: Strings with overlap requiring substitution and deletion ---
Code: levenshtein("flaw", "lawn")

--- Snippet 10 [normal]: Single character substitution (distance 1) ---

--- Snippet 11 [normal]: Reversed strings (distance 2) ---

--- Snippet 12 [normal]: Case sensitivity check (distance 1) ---

--- Snippet 13 [normal]: Unicode/Multi-byte character handling ---

--- Snippet 14 [normal]: Large identical strings to test performance/looping ---

--- Snippet 15 [normal]: Strings with no common characters (total substitution) ---

Code: levenshtein("kitten", "sitting") == levenshtein("sitting", "kitten")
