--- Snippet 1 [normal]: Happy path with simple grouping. ---
Code: group_by([{"a": 1}, {"a": 2}, {"a": 1}], "a")
Output: {1: [{'a': 1}, {'a': 1}], 2: [{'a': 2}]}

--- Snippet 2 [normal]: Empty list of dicts. ---
Code: group_by([], "any_key")
Output: {}

--- Snippet 3 [normal]: Dicts missing the key (should group under None). ---
Code: group_by([{"b": 1}, {"a": 2}], "a")
Output: {None: [{'b': 1}], 2: [{'a': 2}]}

--- Snippet 4 [normal]: Mix of dicts with and without key. ---
Code: group_by([{"type": "fruit"}, {"type": "vegetable"}, {"v": 1}], "type")
Output: {'fruit': [{'type': 'fruit'}], 'vegetable': [{'type': 'vegetable'}], None: [{'v': 1}]}

--- Snippet 5 [normal]: Group by string values. ---
Code: group_by([{"a": "x"}, {"a": "y"}], "a")
Output: {'x': [{'a': 'x'}], 'y': [{'a': 'y'}]}

--- Snippet 6 [normal]: Group by numeric values (integer vs float equivalent). ---
Code: group_by([{"id": 1}, {"id": 1.0}], "id")
Output: {1: [{'id': 1}, {'id': 1.0}]}

--- Snippet 8 [normal]: Group by tuple values. ---
Code: group_by([{"a": (1, 2)}, {"a": (1, 2)}], "a")
Output: {(1, 2): [{'a': (1, 2)}, {'a': (1, 2)}]}

--- Snippet 9 [normal]: Verify that all matching items are included. ---
Code: group_by([{"key": "a"}, {"key": "a"}], "key")
Output: {'a': [{'key': 'a'}, {'key': 'a'}]}

--- Snippet 10 [normal]: Key exists in none of the dicts. ---
Code: group_by([{"key": "val1"}, {"key": "val2"}], "non_existent")
Output: {None: [{'key': 'val1'}, {'key': 'val2'}]}

--- Snippet 11 [normal]: Resulting number of groups. ---
Code: len(group_by([{"a": 1}, {"a": 1}, {"a": 2}], "a"))
Output: 2

--- Snippet 12 [normal]: Explicit None value in dictionary. ---
Code: group_by([{"a": None}, {"a": 1}], "a")
Output: {None: [{'a': None}], 1: [{'a': 1}]}

--- Snippet 13 [normal]: Single dict list. ---
Code: group_by([{"a": 1}], "a")
Output: {1: [{'a': 1}]}

--- Snippet 14 [normal]: Use None as the key (should work if all items lack that key, grouping all under None). ---
Code: group_by([{"a": 1}, {"b": 2}], None)
Output: {None: [{'a': 1}, {'b': 2}]}

--- Snippet 15 [normal]: Large input list. ---
Code: len(group_by([{"k": i} for i in range(100)], "k"))
Output: 100
