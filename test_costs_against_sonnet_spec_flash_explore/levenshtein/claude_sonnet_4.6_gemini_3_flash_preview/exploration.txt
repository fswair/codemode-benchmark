--- Snippet 1 [normal]: Distance between two empty strings (identity) ---
Code: levenshtein("", "")
Output: 0

--- Snippet 2 [normal]: Distance between identical non-empty strings ---
Code: levenshtein("kitten", "kitten")
Output: 0

--- Snippet 3 [normal]: Distance from empty string to non-empty (all insertions) ---
Code: levenshtein("", "hello")
Output: 5

--- Snippet 4 [normal]: Distance from non-empty string to empty (all deletions) ---
Code: levenshtein("world", "")
Output: 5

--- Snippet 5 [normal]: Classic example: kitten to sitting (3 edits) ---
Code: levenshtein("kitten", "sitting")
Output: 3

--- Snippet 6 [normal]: Distance involving both substitution and shift ---
Code: levenshtein("flaw", "lawn")
Output: 2

--- Snippet 7 [normal]: Complete substitution (all characters different) ---
Code: levenshtein("abc", "def")
Output: 3

--- Snippet 8 [normal]: Transposition distance (requires 2 edits: 1 sub + 1 sub or 1 del + 1 ins) ---
Code: levenshtein("ab", "ba")
Output: 2

--- Snippet 9 [normal]: Large difference in string lengths ---
Code: levenshtein("short", "muchlongerstring")
Output: 12

--- Snippet 10 [normal]: Case sensitivity check ---
Code: levenshtein("case", "CASE")
Output: 4

--- Snippet 11 [normal]: Unicode/Emoji support (single substitution) ---
Code: levenshtein("😊", "😂")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 12 [normal]: Unicode composition difference (normalization not handled) ---
Code: levenshtein("cafe", "café")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 13 [normal]: Appending many spaces ---
Code: levenshtein("python", "python" + " " * 10)
Output: 10

--- Snippet 14 [normal]: Large identical strings ---
Code: levenshtein("a" * 100, "a" * 100)
Output: 0

--- Snippet 15 [normal]: Large strings with complete mismatch ---
Code: levenshtein("a" * 50, "b" * 50)
Output: 50

--- Snippet 16 [normal]: Commutativity check: d(s, t) == d(t, s) ---
Code: levenshtein("abc", "acb") == levenshtein("acb", "abc")
Output: True

--- Snippet 21 [normal]: Single character substitution in the middle of the string ---
Code: levenshtein("abc", "axc")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 22 [normal]: Single character insertion at the end (boundary of length) ---
Code: levenshtein("string", "strings")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 23 [normal]: Single character deletion from the end ---
Code: levenshtein("strings", "string")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 24 [normal]: Insertion in the middle of a string ---
Code: levenshtein("ab", "acb")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 25 [normal]: Deletion from the middle of a string ---
Code: levenshtein("acb", "ab")
Output: 1

--- Snippet 26 [normal]: Comparison of different whitespace characters (escape sequences) ---
Code: levenshtein("\n\t", "  ")
Output: 2

--- Snippet 27 [normal]: Shifted similar strings (1 insertion + 1 deletion) ---
Code: levenshtein("A" * 10 + "B", "B" + "A" * 10)
Output: 2

--- Snippet 28 [normal]: Invariant: distance to self is always zero (identity) ---
Code: levenshtein("levenshtein", "levenshtein") == 0
Output: True

--- Snippet 29 [normal]: Upper bound check: distance never exceeds sum of lengths ---
Code: levenshtein("book", "back") <= (len("book") + len("back"))
Output: True

--- Snippet 30 [normal]: Classic example with mixed edits: 3 substitutions/deletions ---
Code: levenshtein("Saturday", "Sunday")
Output: 3

--- Snippet 31 [normal]: Strings containing digit characters only ---
Code: levenshtein("123", "123456789")
Output: 6

--- Snippet 32 [normal]: Stripping punctuation via deletions ---
Code: levenshtein("..!!..", "!!")
Output: 4

--- Snippet 33 [normal]: Asymmetric mismatch: different lengths and different characters ---
Code: levenshtein("a" * 10, "b" * 5)
Output: 10

--- Snippet 34 [normal]: Comparing single space to double space (minimal length difference) ---
Code: levenshtein(" ", "  ")
Output: 1
